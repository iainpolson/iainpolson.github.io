About Me

I've loved music since I was in my early teens, playing in rock bands and trying to record my own projects. I later went onto study Music Production at university.

Following that, I did an internship at Metropolis studios. Since then I have continued to make music and test recording sounds and general audio for games.

My passion is music for media, whether that be video or games. If you require sound design then this is something else that I have studied and I'd love to help you out with projects.
